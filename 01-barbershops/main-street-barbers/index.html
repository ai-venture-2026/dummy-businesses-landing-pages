Our Story
Three generations. One chair. Sixty years of community.
1965

Where It All Began

Grandpa Joe opened the doors at 55 Main Street with nothing but a chair, a razor, and a dream. Word spread fast: this barber treated every customer like family.

1990

The Next Generation

Joe's son Mike took over, bringing modern techniques while keeping the soul of the shop alive. Fathers started bringing their sons -- a tradition that stuck.

Today

Three Generations Strong

Now Mike's daughter Sarah and son Jake run the chairs alongside him. Same family warmth, same quality cuts, same Main Street address. Some things never change.

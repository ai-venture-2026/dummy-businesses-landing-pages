Our Philosophy

We believe that every texture tells a story. From coils to curls, from locs to waves, your hair is a living expression of your heritage, your identity, and your power.

At The Natural Hair Collective, we don't just style hair — we honor it. Our space was built on the belief that specialized care shouldn't be hard to find, and that every person deserves a stylist who truly understands their hair.

Know Your Hair

Texture Guide

Understanding your hair type is the first step to healthier, happier curls. Explore your texture below.

~

Type 2 - Wavy

S-shaped waves that range from fine to coarse. Prone to frizz and can benefit from lightweight moisture.

  • Lightweight Oils
  • Diffusing
  • Anti-Frizz Treatments
  • Gentle Cleansing
S

Type 3A - Loose Curls

Well-defined, springy curls about the width of sidewalk chalk. Can be voluminous and shiny with proper care.

  • Curl Defining Cream
  • Plopping Method
  • Deep Conditioning
  • Satin Pillowcase
@

Type 3C - Tight Curls

Densely packed, corkscrew-shaped curls about the width of a pencil. High volume with lots of personality.

  • Leave-In Conditioner
  • Finger Coiling
  • LOC Method
  • Protective Styling
O

Type 4A - Coily

Tightly coiled S-pattern curls, springy with defined coils. Benefits from generous moisture and gentle handling.

  • Heavy Butters
  • Twist-Outs
  • Moisture Sealing
  • Low Manipulation
Z

Type 4C - Kinky

Tight Z-pattern coils with less defined curl pattern. Most fragile type that thrives with consistent moisture and care.

  • Shea Butter Blends
  • Finger Detangling
  • Hot Oil Treatments
  • Protective Styles

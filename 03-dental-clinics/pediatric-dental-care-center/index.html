Age-by-Age Guide

Dental Care at Every Stage

Every age has unique dental needs. Here's what parents should know!

Babies (0-2 Years)

Starting dental care early sets the foundation for a lifetime of healthy smiles. We recommend your baby's first visit by their first birthday!

  • First dental visit by age 1
  • Gentle gum and tooth examination
  • Teething guidance for parents
  • Bottle and pacifier habit advice
  • Early cavity risk assessment

Toddlers (3-5 Years)

Building great brushing habits starts now! We make dental visits an adventure that your toddler will actually look forward to.

  • Fun, interactive dental cleanings
  • Brushing technique coaching
  • Fluoride varnish treatments
  • Diet and snack guidance
  • Thumb-sucking habit support

Kids (6-12 Years)

As baby teeth make way for permanent ones, proper care is crucial. We keep kids engaged and excited about their dental health!

  • Sealants for new permanent molars
  • Orthodontic monitoring and evaluation
  • Sports guard fitting
  • Cavity prevention education
  • Independence in oral hygiene

Teens (13-17 Years)

Teen years bring unique dental challenges. We provide age-appropriate care that respects their growing independence.

  • Wisdom teeth monitoring
  • Orthodontic treatment coordination
  • Teeth whitening consultations
  • Sports injury prevention
  • Transition to adult dental care

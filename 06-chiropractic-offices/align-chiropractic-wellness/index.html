Conditions We Treat

Find Relief From What's Holding You Back

Our evidence-based chiropractic care addresses a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions with individualized treatment plans tailored to your needs.

Back Pain

From acute lower back injuries to chronic pain, our spinal adjustments and corrective protocols relieve tension and restore mobility.

Neck Pain

Cervical adjustments and soft tissue therapy address stiffness, muscle tension, and restricted range of motion in the neck.

Headaches & Migraines

Cervicogenic headaches and tension headaches often stem from spinal misalignment — chiropractic care provides lasting, drug-free relief.

Sciatica

Targeted lumbar and sacral adjustments relieve pressure on the sciatic nerve, reducing radiating pain, numbness, and tingling in the leg.

Sports Injuries

Athletes recover faster and perform better with chiropractic care addressing sprains, strains, joint dysfunction, and overuse injuries.

Posture Problems

Corrective care protocols and ergonomic guidance address forward head posture, rounded shoulders, and scoliosis for lasting alignment.

Our Approach

Your Wellness Journey in 3 Steps

We don't just treat pain — we build a roadmap to lifelong health with a structured, patient-centered approach.

1

Comprehensive Assessment

We begin with a thorough intake consultation, postural analysis, digital X-rays (when indicated), and neurological evaluation to identify the root cause of your condition — not just the symptoms.

2

Personalized Treatment Plan

Based on your assessment, we design a custom care plan combining spinal adjustments, corrective exercises, nutritional guidance, and therapeutic modalities tailored to your goals and lifestyle.

3

Ongoing Wellness & Maintenance

True health is a journey. Our wellness membership plans keep your spine aligned and your nervous system functioning optimally — preventing problems before they start and supporting lifelong vitality.

Meet the Doctor

Dr. Sarah Mitchell, D.C.

Doctor of Chiropractic · Founder & Lead Practitioner

Dr. Mitchell earned her Doctor of Chiropractic degree from National University of Health Sciences and completed advanced training in corrective care and functional nutrition. With over 15 years of clinical experience and more than 2,000 patients helped, she founded Align Chiropractic Wellness on one belief: every person deserves to live free from pain and full of vitality.

A mother of three and lifelong advocate for family wellness, Dr. Mitchell takes a deeply personal approach to care — treating each patient as a whole person, not just a set of symptoms. She specializes in corrective chiropractic care, pediatric chiropractic, and integrating nutrition into long-term wellness plans.

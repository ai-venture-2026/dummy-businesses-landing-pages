What We Treat

Conditions We Address

Modern professional life creates predictable injury patterns. We specialize in diagnosing and resolving the conditions most common to office and executive lifestyles.

Text Neck

Cervical strain from prolonged screen use — relieved through targeted soft tissue work and spinal realignment.

Lower Back Pain

The leading cause of lost productivity. Our lumbar protocols restore mobility and eliminate chronic discomfort fast.

Carpal Tunnel

Wrist and hand nerve compression from repetitive typing — treated with soft tissue therapy and nerve mobilization.

Tension Headaches

Cervicogenic and stress-driven headaches resolved through upper cervical adjustments and muscle release therapy.

Poor Posture

Structural and habitual postural dysfunction corrected through our evidence-based Posture Correction Protocol.

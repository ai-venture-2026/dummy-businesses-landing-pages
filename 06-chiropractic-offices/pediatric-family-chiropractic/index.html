Specialized Certification

The Webster Technique — Certified Prenatal Chiropractic

The Webster Technique is a specific chiropractic sacral analysis and diversified adjustment developed by Dr. Larry Webster. Our doctor is certified by the International Chiropractic Pediatric Association (ICPA) in this specialized method.

This technique focuses on reducing the effects of sacral subluxation and sacroiliac joint dysfunction, restoring proper pelvic balance and function for optimal fetal positioning.

What We Treat

Evidence-based care designed for athletic performance and injury recovery.

Athletic Adjustments

Sport-specific spinal and joint adjustments to restore mobility and support peak output.

  • Biomechanical analysis
  • Spinal & extremity mobilization
  • Pre/post-game protocols

Injury Rehabilitation

Targeted rehab for sprains, strains, and overuse injuries to get you back in the game.

  • Acute injury treatment
  • Soft tissue therapy (ART, IASTM)
  • Return-to-sport clearance

Performance Optimization

Unlock greater power, speed, and endurance through optimized musculoskeletal function.

  • Functional movement screening
  • Neuromuscular re-education
  • Season maintenance plans

Movement Assessment

Full-body screening to identify compensations before they become injuries.

  • FMS & SFMA screening
  • Gait & running analysis
  • Personalized corrective plan

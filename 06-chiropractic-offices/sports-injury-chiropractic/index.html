What We Treat

Evidence-based chiropractic services designed around the demands of athletic performance and injury recovery.

Athletic Adjustments

Sport-specific spinal and extremity adjustments that restore optimal joint function, improve range of motion, and support peak athletic output.

  • Sport-specific biomechanical analysis
  • Spinal & extremity mobilization
  • Pre-game preparation protocols
  • Post-competition recovery care

Injury Rehabilitation

Comprehensive rehab programs targeting sports injuries — from acute strains and sprains to chronic overuse conditions — getting you back in the game fast.

  • Acute injury assessment & treatment
  • Therapeutic exercise programming
  • Soft tissue therapy (ART, IASTM)
  • Return-to-sport clearance protocols

Performance Optimization

Go beyond pain relief. Our performance protocols help healthy athletes unlock greater power, speed, and endurance through optimized musculoskeletal function.

  • Functional movement screening
  • Neuromuscular re-education
  • Speed & power enhancement drills
  • Sport-season maintenance plans

Movement Assessment

Full-body biomechanical screening that identifies compensations and movement dysfunctions before they become injuries — essential for every serious athlete.

  • FMS & SFMA screening
  • Gait & running analysis
  • Posture & alignment evaluation
  • Personalized corrective plan
Proven Process

Our 48-Hour Recovery Protocol

A structured, time-tested pathway from injury to peak performance — built around the athlete's schedule.

1
Day 1

Assessment

Comprehensive biomechanical evaluation, injury history review, and sport-specific movement screening. We identify root causes, not just symptoms.

2
Week 1

Treatment

Targeted adjustments, soft tissue therapy, and therapeutic modalities to reduce pain, restore mobility, and begin the healing cascade.

3
Weeks 2–3

Rehab

Progressive rehabilitative exercise, neuromuscular retraining, and sport-specific movement patterns to rebuild strength and stability.

4
Month 1+

Performance

Return to sport with optimized function. Ongoing maintenance care and performance protocols to prevent re-injury and elevate your game.

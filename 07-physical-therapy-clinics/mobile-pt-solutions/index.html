What We Offer

Specialized Care,
Delivered to You

Post-Surgery Rehab

Recovering from hip replacement, knee surgery, or spinal procedures? Our therapists design home-based protocols that match your surgical team's guidelines. We track progress meticulously and coordinate with your surgeon to ensure optimal outcomes — all without you leaving home.

Schedule a session →

Balance & Fall Prevention

Falls are the leading cause of injury for adults over 65. Our therapists assess your home environment, identify hazards, and build a personalized strength and balance program. We train you in the exact spaces where falls are most likely to occur — your own home.

Learn more →

Pain Management

Chronic back pain, arthritis, neuropathy — we address it all with evidence-based manual therapy, therapeutic exercise, and education. No medications, no invasive procedures. Just skilled hands and proven techniques in the comfort of your own space.

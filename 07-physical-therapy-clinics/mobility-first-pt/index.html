We Treat

Conditions We Specialize In

Our therapists have advanced training in the conditions most commonly affecting older adults.

Balance Problems

Personalized vestibular and proprioceptive training to restore your steadiness and walking confidence.

Fall Prevention

Evidence-based programs that identify your personal risk factors and strengthen the muscles that protect you from falling.

Hip & Knee Pain

Conservative, low-impact treatment to reduce joint pain, improve range of motion, and help you move freely again.

Post-Surgery Recovery

Guided rehabilitation after hip replacement, knee surgery, or cardiac procedures — restoring strength and independence.

Arthritis

Specialized joint protection strategies, gentle strengthening, and pain management techniques for osteo- and rheumatoid arthritis.

Parkinson's Disease

LSVT BIG-certified therapy and targeted motor training to improve gait, coordination, and daily function.

Our Process

How We Help You Thrive

A clear, compassionate process from your very first call to your last session — and beyond.

1

Comprehensive Evaluation

We begin with a thorough assessment of your strength, balance, mobility, and pain — tailored to your specific age-related needs and goals. Your FREE fall risk assessment happens here.

2

Personalized Care Plan

3

Progress & Independence

In-Home Physical Therapy

We Come to You

For patients who are homebound, recently discharged from hospital, or simply unable to travel — our licensed therapists bring the full power of our clinic directly to your home.

  • Medicare-covered in-home therapy for qualifying homebound patients
  • Therapy adapted to your actual home environment — stairs, bathrooms, and routines
  • Same expert therapists — no less care because you're at home
  • Home safety assessments and fall hazard identification included
  • Flexible scheduling — mornings, afternoons, and some weekends available

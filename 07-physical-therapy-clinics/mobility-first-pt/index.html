Conditions We Treat

Specialized Care for Every Challenge

Scroll through the conditions we treat most — each with a tailored, evidence-based approach.

Balance Problems

Comprehensive vestibular rehabilitation and balance retraining using state-of-the-art assessment tools to restore your confidence in everyday movement.

Fall Prevention

Targeted strength, flexibility, and coordination training designed to dramatically reduce your risk of falling and the fear that comes with it.

Hip & Knee Pain

Gentle mobilization and progressive strengthening for joint pain, helping you walk, climb stairs, and move through daily life without discomfort.

Post-Surgery Recovery

Structured rehabilitation after hip replacement, knee replacement, or other orthopedic procedures to get you back on your feet safely and quickly.

Arthritis

Pain-relieving manual therapy, aquatic exercises, and joint protection strategies that preserve mobility and improve quality of life with arthritis.

Parkinson's Disease

LSVT BIG-certified therapy focusing on amplitude training, gait improvement, and maintaining independence through movement-specific exercises.

Our Approach

Three Steps to Greater Mobility

A clear, compassionate process designed to help you regain confidence in your movement.

01

Comprehensive Evaluation

We start with a thorough assessment of your balance, strength, mobility, and daily challenges — taking the time to truly understand your situation.

02

Personalized Care Plan

Your treatment plan is crafted specifically for your goals, whether that's walking to the mailbox or dancing at a grandchild's wedding.

03

Progress & Independence

We track your improvement with measurable milestones and equip you with exercises and strategies to maintain your gains long after therapy ends.

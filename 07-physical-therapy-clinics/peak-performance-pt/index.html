Your Path Forward

The Recovery Journey

A structured, science-backed four-phase program that gets you from pain and limitation to full competitive performance.

1
Evaluation
Week 1

Comprehensive movement screening, strength testing, and functional assessment. We establish your baseline and design your personalized recovery plan.

2
Pain Relief
Weeks 2–4

Manual therapy, dry needling, and targeted modalities reduce pain and inflammation. Gentle mobility work restores range of motion and tissue health.

3
Rebuilding Strength
Weeks 4–10

Progressive strength training, neuromuscular re-education, and sport-specific conditioning. Blood flow restriction training accelerates muscle rebuilding.

4
Return to Sport
Weeks 10+

Objective performance testing, sport-specific movement drills, and clearance testing. Return to competition with confidence — stronger and more resilient than before.

Advanced Treatment Methods

Technology That Accelerates Recovery

We invest in the same techniques used by professional sports organizations to give every athlete access to elite-level treatment.

Blood Flow Restriction

BFR training allows athletes to build strength and muscle mass at significantly lower loads — critical during early post-surgical phases when heavy loading is not yet safe. Achieve 70% of the strength gains with 20% of the weight.

Faster Muscle Recovery
Dry Needling

Trigger point dry needling targets myofascial pain, breaks down scar tissue, and restores normal muscle function. Particularly effective for chronic sports injuries, post-surgical tightness, and movement pattern dysfunction.

Pain Relief & Tissue Healing
Performance Testing

Force plate analysis, isokinetic strength testing, hop testing protocols, and video biomechanics provide objective data at every stage. We do not guess when you are ready to return — we test it with measurable standards.

Our Services

Specialized Care for Every Recovery

Evidence-based treatments tailored to your unique needs and recovery timeline.

Post-Surgical Rehab

Accelerated recovery protocols for joint replacements, ACL repairs, rotator cuff surgery, and more. Guided from day one.

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques including joint mobilization, soft tissue work, and myofascial release to restore mobility and reduce pain.

Sports Rehab

Return-to-sport programming with sport-specific movement analysis, strength building, and performance benchmarks.

Chronic Pain Management

Multidisciplinary approach combining movement therapy, education, and graded exposure to break the cycle of persistent pain.

Dry Needling

Trigger point therapy using thin needles to relieve muscular tension and improve range of motion.

Balance & Fall Prevention

Vestibular rehab and stability training to reduce fall risk and improve confidence in movement.

Work Injury Recovery

Functional capacity evaluations and work-conditioning programs to get you back on the job safely.

Telehealth PT

Virtual sessions for exercise guidance, progress check-ins, and home program management.

Recovery Roadmap

Your Path From Injury to Full Function

A structured, milestone-based approach so you always know where you are — and what comes next.

Week 1–2
Assessment & Pain Control
Comprehensive evaluation, goal-setting, pain reduction, and initial mobility work.
Week 3–4
Range of Motion
Restore joint mobility, reduce scar tissue, activate stabilizing muscles.
Week 5–6
Strength Building
Progressive resistance training, neuromuscular re-education, and endurance work.
Week 7–8
Functional Training
Activity-specific movements, balance and coordination, return-to-work tasks.
Week 9+
Full Return
Sport or activity clearance, home program mastery, and long-term maintenance plan.

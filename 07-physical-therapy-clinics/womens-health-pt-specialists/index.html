How It Works

Your Path to Recovery

A thoughtful, step-by-step approach designed to meet you exactly where you are and guide you toward lasting wellness.

1

Initial Consultation

A private, unhurried conversation where we listen to your story, understand your concerns, and discuss your goals in a safe, supportive setting.

2

Assessment & Plan

A thorough physical evaluation followed by a personalized treatment plan tailored specifically to your body, your needs, and your lifestyle.

3

Treatment Sessions

Hands-on therapy combined with guided exercises in your private room. We progress at your pace, adjusting your plan as you grow stronger.

4

Recovery & Wellness

As you reach your goals, we equip you with home exercises and wellness strategies so you can maintain your progress and thrive independently.

Why Choose Us

Built for the Community, Not for Profit

Every decision we make is guided by our mission: accessible, transparent, compassionate veterinary care for every pet and every family.

Nonprofit Mission

We are a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Every dollar above operating cost goes back into reduced fees, community programs, and expanded services. No shareholders. No profits. Just pets.

Transparent Pricing

Every price is posted publicly and updated regularly. You'll know exactly what you're paying before your pet is ever seen. No estimates that balloon into bills.

No Hidden Fees

What's on our price list is what's on your invoice. We never add surprise charges, "facility fees," or mandatory upsells. The price you see is the price you pay, period.

Community Partnerships

We partner with local shelters, food banks, housing agencies, and community organizations to reach the families who need us most. Vouchers, mobile clinics, and outreach events year-round.

Financial Assistance

We Believe Every Pet Deserves Care

We know that sometimes money is tight. Our assistance programs make sure your pet's health is never out of reach, no matter your situation.

Sliding Scale Fee Program

Fees adjusted based on household income and family size. Bring proof of income or benefits enrollment and we'll work with you to find a price that fits your budget.

Community Voucher Programs

We partner with local food banks, housing programs, and social service agencies to distribute free or reduced-cost veterinary care vouchers to families in need.

Senior & Fixed Income Discount

Additional 15% discount for seniors (65+) and families on fixed income. Also available for active military, veterans, and first responders with ID.

No-Cost Emergency Consultations

If your pet needs emergency guidance and you cannot afford a visit, call us. We offer free phone triage and will direct you to the best resources available in our area.

Our Services

Comprehensive Care for Every Stage of Life

From puppy and kitten checkups to senior wellness plans, we provide expert care with a gentle, compassionate approach.

Wellness Exams

Annual and bi-annual nose-to-tail physical exams to keep your pet healthy at every age. Includes nutritional counseling and preventive care guidance.

Vaccinations

Core and lifestyle vaccines tailored to your pet's individual needs, age, and risk factors. We keep your companions protected and on schedule.

Dental Care

Professional dental cleanings, digital dental x-rays, extractions, and at-home dental care education to prevent painful periodontal disease.

Surgery

Soft tissue surgical procedures including spay/neuter, mass removals, and laceration repairs performed with advanced anesthetic monitoring and pain management.

In-house digital radiography and ultrasound for rapid, accurate diagnosis. We provide same-day results so we can begin treatment without delay.

Our Approach

Veterinary Care
Without the Fear

Fear-Free certification is the gold standard in veterinary medicine, ensuring that every visit is as stress-free as possible for your pet. We understand that a frightened pet is harder to examine — and a stressed pet takes longer to heal.

Our entire team — from reception to exam room — is trained to read your pet's body language, move at their pace, and use positive reinforcement at every step.

Calm Environment

Separate dog and cat waiting areas, calming pheromones, soft lighting

Gentle Handling

Low-stress techniques, no scruffing, patient-led pacing throughout

Positive Reinforcement

High-value treats, praise, and reward-based associations with the clinic

Certified Team

Every staff member trained and certified in fear-free best practices

Senior Pet Care Program

Golden Years
Deserve Golden Care

Senior pets — dogs over 7 and cats over 10 — benefit enormously from specialized care designed around their unique needs. Our Senior Pet Care Program catches age-related changes early, when they're most treatable, giving your companion the longest and most comfortable life possible.

Enrollment includes a comprehensive baseline exam, bloodwork panel, urinalysis, blood pressure check, and a personalized wellness plan updated every six months.

Bi-Annual Comprehensive Exams

Twice-yearly nose-to-tail exams catch subtle changes before they become serious conditions.

Senior Bloodwork Panel

Full chemistry profile, CBC, thyroid, and kidney/liver function to monitor organ health over time.

Arthritis & Pain Management

Assessment and multimodal pain management plans including medications, joint supplements, and lifestyle adjustments.

Quality-of-Life Assessments

Ongoing conversations about your pet's happiness, comfort, and wellbeing at every stage of their senior years.

Your Teachers

Meet Our Instructors

Certified, passionate, and devoted to helping you grow — on and off the mat.

Maya Chen

RYT-500  |  E-RYT 200
Vinyasa Flow & Yin

With 12 years of teaching experience and training in India and Bali, Maya brings a seamless blend of traditional Hatha roots with dynamic modern Vinyasa. Her classes are challenging, joyful, and deeply centering.

Sara Levy

Pure Barre Certified  |  ACE-CPT
Barre Fusion & Pilates

A former professional dancer turned certified barre and fitness instructor, Sara's classes are equal parts sculpting and celebrating your body. Her high-energy instruction keeps you motivated class after class.

Priya Nair

RYT-200  |  Certified Meditation Teacher
Restorative & Meditation

Priya's gentle, grounding presence creates a sanctuary in every session. Trained in mindfulness-based stress reduction (MBSR) and restorative yoga, she guides students toward profound stillness and self-compassion.

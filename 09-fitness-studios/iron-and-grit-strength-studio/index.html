What We Train

OUR PROGRAMS

Four focused programs. Every one built around the barbell. No fluff — just structured training that delivers results on the platform.

01
Powerlifting Foundations

Master the squat, bench, and deadlift from the ground up. Technical coaching meets progressive overload in a structured 12-week block system.

  • Beginner & intermediate levels
  • Technical cue-heavy coaching
  • Meet introduction optional
  • 4 days per week programming
02
Olympic Weightlifting

Snatch and clean & jerk technique built from scratch or refined at advanced levels. Coached by nationally-certified weightlifting coaches.

  • Snatch & C&J technique
  • USAW-certified coaching
  • Competition pathway available
  • 5 sessions per week access
03
Competition Prep

Periodized peaking cycles tailored to your meet date. Weight management, opener selection, attempt strategy — total meet-day readiness.

  • Meet-specific peaking cycles
  • Weight class management
  • Attempt selection strategy
  • Coach at meet (optional add-on)
04
1-on-1 Strength Coaching

Direct, personalized coaching from our head strength coaches. Program design, form analysis, and hands-on instruction every session.

  • Fully customized programming
  • Video analysis & feedback
  • Nutrition guidance available
  • Direct coach communication

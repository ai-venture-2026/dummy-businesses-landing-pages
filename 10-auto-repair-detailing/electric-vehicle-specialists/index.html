EVs Require Different Expertise

Electric vehicles share nothing under the hood with gas cars. Battery chemistry, high-voltage architecture, power electronics, and software systems demand technicians trained specifically on EV technology — not converted mechanics.

Dealers are overwhelmed with EV service demand. We maintain dedicated EV bays, specialized diagnostic equipment, and a team focused exclusively on electric drivetrains — meaning same-day and next-day availability when dealers quote weeks.

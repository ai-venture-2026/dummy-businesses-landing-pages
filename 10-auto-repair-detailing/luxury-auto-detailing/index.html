The Method

Our Meticulous 47-Step Process

Every vehicle follows the same uncompromising protocol. Nothing is skipped. Nothing is rushed.

01–05

Initial Assessment

Paint thickness mapping, condition documentation, swirl analysis under LED lighting, and full photographic record.

06–12

Pre-Wash Protocol

Wheel wells, underbody, and panel gaps treated with pH-neutral snow foam. All exhaust and trim areas protected.

13–18

Paint Decontamination

Iron fallout removal, tar and adhesive dissolution, and clay bar treatment to achieve a perfectly clean surface.

19–25

Two-Bucket Wash

Premium pH-balanced soap, microfiber wash mitts, and dedicated wheels with separate tools to prevent cross-contamination.

26–32

Machine Polishing

Multi-stage cutting and finishing compounds remove imperfections panel by panel. Each section checked under LED before moving forward.

33–38

Interior Restoration

Full extraction, leather pH-balanced cleaning, alcantara brush technique, glass polishing, and compressed air detailing of vents.

39–43

Protection Application

Ceramic coating, sealant, or wax applied in controlled environment. Panel by panel. Cure time strictly observed.

44–47

Final Inspection

Full walk-around inspection under raking LED light. Final photography. Certificate of completion issued. Owner walkthrough.

Sedation Options

From Mild to Deep -- You Choose

We offer a range of sedation levels so you can find the perfect fit for your comfort. Every option is safe, monitored, and administered with care.

Nitrous Oxide

Mild
How It Works

Breathe in a gentle mix of nitrous oxide and oxygen through a small nose mask. Effects wear off within minutes.

You'll Feel

Light, floaty, and relaxed. You're fully awake and can communicate easily.

Best For

Mild anxiety, shorter procedures, first-time visitors wanting a gentle introduction.

Oral Sedation

Moderate
How It Works

Take a prescribed pill before your appointment. You'll feel deeply relaxed while remaining conscious.

You'll Feel

Drowsy and calm. You may not remember much of the procedure -- and that's perfectly okay.

Best For

Moderate anxiety, longer procedures, patients who want minimal awareness.

IV Sedation

Deep
How It Works

Sedation is delivered through an IV, allowing precise control. You'll be in a deep state of relaxation throughout.

You'll Feel

In a dream-like state. Most patients feel as though the procedure lasted only moments.

Best For

Severe dental phobia, extensive treatments, patients who haven't visited a dentist in years.

Not sure which option is right for you? We will help you choose during your free comfort consultation.

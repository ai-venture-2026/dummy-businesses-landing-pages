Your Journey

Trimester-by-Trimester Guide

Every stage of pregnancy brings unique needs. We've tailored our services to support you at every step.

First Trimester (Weeks 1-12)

The beginning of your beautiful journey. Gentle treatments focused on easing early pregnancy symptoms and promoting relaxation during this transformative time.

  • Gentle relaxation massage (light pressure)
  • Aromatherapy with pregnancy-safe essential oils
  • Nausea relief acupressure techniques
  • Stress and anxiety reduction sessions
Safety Note: All first trimester sessions use only pregnancy-safe techniques. We always begin with a comprehensive health consultation.

Second Trimester (Weeks 13-26)

Often called the "golden trimester." Your energy is returning and your bump is growing. Our treatments adapt to your changing body and help you feel your best.

  • Side-lying prenatal massage with body pillows
  • Lower back and sciatic pain relief
  • Gentle stretching and flexibility sessions
  • Prenatal yoga introduction class
Safety Note: We use specialized pregnancy cushions and side-lying positions. All techniques avoid pressure points associated with uterine stimulation.

Third Trimester (Weeks 27-40)

The home stretch! Your body is working hard. Our third trimester services focus on comfort, swelling relief, and preparing your body for labor and delivery.

  • Edema (swelling) reduction massage
  • Hip and pelvic comfort techniques
  • Labor preparation massage
  • Gentle pelvic floor awareness sessions
Safety Note: Third trimester sessions are shorter and feature elevated positioning. We monitor your comfort continuously throughout.

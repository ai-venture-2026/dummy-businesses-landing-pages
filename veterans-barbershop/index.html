Our Story

Veterans Barbershop was founded in 2010 by two Army veterans who saw a need in their community. After years of service, they wanted to create a space where military members, veterans, and their families could feel at home.

What started as a two-chair shop on Freedom Boulevard has grown into a cornerstone of the military community. Our barbers are trained in every regulation cut across all branches, and many are veterans themselves.

We believe in the same values we served under: discipline, respect, precision, and brotherhood. Every cut is delivered with that standard, and every customer leaves looking sharp and feeling respected.

Serving Our Community

Veterans Barbershop is more than a place to get a haircut. We are a hub for the military community, dedicated to giving back to those who have given so much.

  • Free haircuts for homeless veterans on the first Saturday of every month
  • Annual fundraiser for local VA hospital programs
  • Partnership with veteran transition and employment programs
  • Sponsor of local VFW post and Memorial Day events
  • Back-to-school cuts for military children every August
